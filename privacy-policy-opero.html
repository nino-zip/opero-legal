Opero respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use the Opero mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Opero does not require you to create an account and does not collect personal information such as email addresses, phone numbers, or names for identification purposes.

The app may use the following locally stored information:

  • A display name and avatar emoji chosen by the user
  • App preferences (such as theme color and reminders)
  • Practice progress and session results

All of this data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

2. Microphone and Speech Recognition

Opero may request access to the device microphone and speech recognition features to allow users to answer math questions using their voice.

Audio input is processed using Apple-provided APIs. Opero does not store, record, or transmit voice data.

3. Notifications

Opero may send local notifications to remind users to practice math. These notifications are scheduled and managed entirely on the device and do not involve any external servers.

4. Data Sharing

Opero does not sell, share, or transfer user data to third parties.

5. Analytics and Tracking

Opero does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.

6. Children’s Privacy

Opero is designed to be safe for users of all ages. The app does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected within the app or on the official policy page.
